    The Oregon Water Atlas is an aggregation and visualization of Oregonâ€™s water data. It was created as a response to the wide array of publicly available data scatted across the Internet. The Oregon Water Atlas was created using entirely open-source software and coding libraries, including QGIS, Leaflet, storymap.js, and Bootstrap.
